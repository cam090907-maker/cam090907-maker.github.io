PhD Student in Natural Language Processing

Researching state-of-the-art AI methods in NLP and Multimodal Large Language Models at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI)

I graduated in Computer Science from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology - Vietnam National University (HCMUT-VNU) in an accelerated 3.5 years, and I am now pursuing a PhD in NLP at MBZUAI. Throughout my undergraduate journey under the guidance of Assoc. Prof. Dr. Tho T. Quan, I built strong foundations in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Multimodal Analysis, with programming experience in Python, C++, and Java.

My current interests focus on vision-language models, uncertainty estimation, and multimodal learning.
